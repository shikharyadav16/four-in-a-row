Four in a Row:

The classic strategy game where you try to connect four pieces in a row!

How to Play:

  • 🎮 Two players take turns dropping pieces into a 6x7 grid.
  • 🔴🟢 The goal is to connect four of your pieces in a row.
  • ↘️ You can win horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • 🚀 Plan your moves to block your opponent and set up a win.
  • 🏆 First player to connect four wins!
